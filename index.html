Our Process

The MAISON Design Journey

01

Discovery Consultation

We begin by understanding your lifestyle, aesthetic vision, and aspirations through an immersive conversation.

02

Site Analysis

Our team conducts a detailed survey of your space — measurements, light studies, and spatial assessment.

03

Concept Development

We craft bespoke mood boards, spatial layouts, and design narratives that reflect your unique personality.

04

3D Visualization

Photorealistic renders allow you to experience your future space before a single element is installed.

05

Material Curation

Select from our curated library of premium stone, textiles, metals, and artisan finishes.

06

Execution & Reveal

Our skilled craftsmen bring the vision to life with meticulous attention to detail and a white-glove handover.
